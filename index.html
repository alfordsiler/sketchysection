About SketchySection

Above is a heatmap showing street sections in the Seattle area where police have responded to traffic related incidents over the past 6 years. The purpose of this display is provide a quick visual representation to help the public determine if a suspected area is dangerous and possibly in need of some type of traffic revision/signage.

Want to alert the Seattle Department of Transportation? You can submit a general inquiry here: SDOT

Data for this website is provided by a Socrata API which can be found here: Seattle Traffic Accidents. Socrata is a company who works to make government data more easily accessible to the public and usable by Developers. Learn more about what they do here: Socrata
